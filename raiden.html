History

Raiden, real name Jack, is a former soldier and special operations agent. He is known for his role as the main protagonist of Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty, where he was initially a newcomer to the series and the player's controllable character.

Raiden was born in Liberia in the 1980s and was orphaned at a young age during the Liberian Civil War. He was rescued by the Colossus Project, a private military company, and was trained to be a child soldier. After being rescued by the NGO, Save the Children, Raiden was taken to the United States where he was adopted and raised in a peaceful environment.

As an adult, Raiden joined the U.S. military and became a member of the special operations unit, FOXHOUND. He was chosen by Solid Snake to participate in the mission to stop the terrorist organization, the Sons of Liberty, and their plan to use the new Metal Gear, the Arsenal Gear, to take over the world. Raiden played a key role in the mission's success and later went on to have a successful career as a special operations agent.
